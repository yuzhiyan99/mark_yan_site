I am an Assistant Professor in Accounting at Hong Kong Baptist University. I joined HKBU in 2021 after completing my Ph.D. in Accounting at the University of Arizona.

My research focuses on financial accounting, corporate disclosure, debt contracting, banking relationships, and managerial behavior. My work has appeared in _Contemporary Accounting Research_ and the _Journal of Corporate Finance_.
